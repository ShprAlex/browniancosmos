About

BrownianCosmos is an exploration of patterns present in Brownian motion.

Surprisingly, simple randomness does not result in a perfectly even distribution, but rather produces fractal clumps of density at every scale. In a system of particles under Brownian motion these regions of density shift in a continuous wavelike way.

This simulation reveals Brownian density waves by converting differences in the number of particles between adjacent interval lengths to color wavelength and intensity, letting us better appreciate the mathematics and beauty of this phenomenon.
